About BLOODMONEY!

BLOODMONEY! is a chilling and twisted simulation game that combines clicker mechanics with horror and RPG elements. Players enter a grim world where every click generates profit, but at a growing moral cost. The game challenges you to balance greed and conscience, while eerie visuals and a tense atmosphere create a haunting, unforgettable experience.

Story Overview

Created by SHROOMYCHRIST and released on August 2, 2025, on itch.io, the game tells the unsettling tale of Harvey. Beneath its seemingly pastel-colored presentation lies a story of desperation, moral compromise, and psychological descent. Every decision forces players to consider how far they are willing to go for survival, with consequences that linger long after the game ends.

Core Gameplay

  • Click to Earn: Continuously attack Harvey to generate money, escalating chaos and dark humor with every action.

  • Upgrade Strategically: Purchase upgrades that increase both profits and the intensity of Harvey’s suffering, creating a delicate balance between cruelty and gain.

  • Unlock Multiple Endings: Your choices shape one of three outcomes—Good, Bad, or Normal Ending—offering replay value and moral experimentation.

  • Visual Transformation: Watch as Harvey changes under the weight of each upgrade, reflecting the consequences of your decisions.

Controls

  • Mouse Click: Use simple clicks to progress through the game, trigger actions, and manage upgrades.

BLOODMONEY! offers a provocative mix of horror, strategy, and dark humor, testing players’ limits in a disturbing yet compelling way.
